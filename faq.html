FAQ

Do you have questions? We have answers!

Who is Urban E Recycling? Urban E Recycling, Inc., was founded and continues to be managed by an environmentally conscientious team of professionals. We love serving the Greater Tampa Bay Area. We follow a strict No Landfill policy.

Is recycling my electronics free? Yes, when you use us, recycling electronics is free to the Greater Tampa Bay Area. Urban E Recycling will pick-up electronic waste from commercial & residential locations. The warehouse is also available for drop offs, during business hours.

What happens to my electronics once they are picked up? Once the equipment arrives at our warehouse your items will be sorted, dismantled and then processed for recycling. All data storage media will be shredded to ensure your information is protected.
